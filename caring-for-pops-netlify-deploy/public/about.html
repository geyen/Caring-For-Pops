About Caring for Pops

We're on a mission to revolutionize how families find trusted home health care for their loved ones.

Our Mission

To transform how families find and connect with quality home health care providers through a compassionate, efficient, and technology-driven approach.

Our Leadership Team

Lyndsay Geyen

Founder & Administrator

Lyndsay Geyen is a visionary entrepreneur and advocate for quality home health care, dedicated to transforming how agencies connect with clients in need. With a Master's in Education and a deep understanding of human connection, she founded Caring for Pops to bridge the gap between compassionate caregivers and the families who need them most.

Driven by a passion for fairness and efficiency, Lyndsay saw a broken system—where agencies paid premium prices for leads shared with countless competitors—and set out to disrupt the industry. Her innovative approach ensures that every lead is exclusive, giving agencies a real opportunity to serve those in need while maintaining integrity and value.

At Caring for Pops, Lyndsay leads with a commitment to exceptional service, ethical business practices, and a deep respect for the families and professionals she serves. Her expertise in recruiting, retention, and operational excellence has made her a trusted leader in the home health care space, ensuring that both caregivers and clients receive the support they deserve.

Christopher Geyen

Lead Sales

As Lead Sales at Caring for Pops, Christopher Geyen plays a vital role in connecting quality home care providers with families in need. With a deep understanding of the home care industry and its unique challenges, he focuses on building strong relationships with providers who share our commitment to exceptional care.

Christopher's approach to sales is built on transparency, authenticity, and a genuine desire to help home care agencies grow their businesses through our exclusive lead model. He recognizes that successful partnerships require trust and mutual benefit, which guides his interactions with every provider in our network.

Working closely with the leadership team, Christopher ensures that our provider network continues to expand with high-quality agencies that can deliver the level of care our families deserve. His dedication to our mission helps strengthen Caring for Pops' position as an innovator in the home health care connection space.

Our Core Values

Compassion

Every decision we make considers the dignity, comfort, and well-being of those receiving care and those providing it.

Integrity

We maintain the highest ethical standards in all our operations, from provider screening to client interactions.

Innovation

We continuously seek better ways to connect families with care providers, leveraging technology to improve the care experience.

Personalized Matching

Creating genuine connections between caregivers and clients based on needs, preferences, and personalities.

Quality Assurance

Maintaining rigorous standards through comprehensive screening, verification, and ongoing evaluation.

Accessibility

Making quality care accessible to all families regardless of location or socioeconomic status.
