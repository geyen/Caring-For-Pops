Privacy Policy

Caring for Pops - Home Health Care Lead Generation Platform

Effective Date: April 9, 2025

At Caring for Pops, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our lead generation platform.

1. Information We Collect

We collect several types of information from and about users of our platform, including:

  • Personal Identifiers: Name, email address, phone number, postal address, and payment information.
  • Care-Related Information: For care seekers, information about care needs, health conditions, and preferences.
  • Provider Information: For providers, information about services offered, location, certification, and business details.
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, device information, and cookies.
  • Usage Data: How you interact with our platform, pages visited, and features used.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for various purposes, including:

  • Matching care seekers with appropriate providers based on needs and location
  • Processing payments and managing subscriptions
  • Communicating with you about our services
  • Improving our platform and developing new features
  • Sending administrative notifications and updates
  • Ensuring compliance with our terms and policies
  • Preventing fraud and enhancing security

3. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We may share your information in the following contexts:

  • Between Users: When care seekers and providers are matched, relevant contact and care information is shared.
  • Service Providers: We work with third-party service providers who help us operate our business (payment processors, email providers, analytics services).
  • Business Transfers: If Caring for Pops is involved in a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required by law or in response to valid legal requests.

4. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access to your personal information
  • Correction of inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Deletion of your personal information
  • Restriction of processing or objection to processing
  • Data portability
  • Withdrawal of consent

To exercise these rights, please contact us at privacy@caringforpops.com.

6. Third-Party Services

Our platform may include links to third-party websites or services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of such third parties, and we encourage you to review their privacy policies.

7. Children's Privacy

Our service is not directed to children under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18. If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child under 18, we will take steps to delete such information.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Effective Date." We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.
